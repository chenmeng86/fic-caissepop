Request for Standing Offer - BULK AVIATION FUELS
National Master Standing Offers (NMSO) for a Back-up supplier for bulk aviation fuels in accordance with Canadian General Standards Board standards, as applicable. Delivery to Canadian Forces Bases Val-Cartier, Bagotville, Trenton, Winnipeg, and Comox, on an "as and when" requested basis from date of issue of the MNSO to March 31, 2013. 
Quantity: various.
The Crown reserves the right to negotiate with suppliers on any procurement.
Documents may be submitted in either official language of Canada.
